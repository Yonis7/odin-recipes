Lasanga

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish consisting of layers of flat pasta sheets, alternating with a rich meat or vegetable-based sauce and creamy cheese such as ricotta or mozzarella. The layers are then baked in the oven until the cheese on top is melted and bubbly, creating a deliciously comforting and hearty meal. Lasagna is a versatile dish that can be made with a variety of fillings and sauces, making it a popular comfort food in many parts of the world.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna pasta sheets
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ground beef or Italian sausage (optional)
  • Onion, chopped
  • Garlic, minced
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • Parmesan cheese, grated
  • Eggs, beaten
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Olive oil

Directions / Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna pasta sheets according to package instructions, then drain and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet over medium heat, cook the ground beef or Italian sausage until browned, breaking it up into small pieces as it cooks.
  4. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic to the skillet, and cook until the onion is translucent and the garlic is fragrant.
  5. Add the tomato sauce to the skillet, and season with salt and pepper to taste. Let the sauce simmer for a few minutes until it has thickened slightly.
  6. In a mixing bowl, combine the beaten eggs, ricotta cheese, and grated Parmesan cheese.
  7. Grease a 9x13 inch baking dish with olive oil, then spread a thin layer of the meat sauce on the bottom of the dish.
  8. Place a layer of cooked lasagna pasta sheets on top of the meat sauce, then spread a layer of the ricotta cheese mixture on top of the pasta sheets.
  9. Add another layer of meat sauce on top of the ricotta cheese, followed by another layer of pasta sheets.
  10. Repeat layering with the remaining meat sauce, pasta sheets, and ricotta cheese mixture, ending with a layer of meat sauce on top.
  11. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese on top of the meat sauce layer.
  12. Cover the baking dish with foil, and bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes.
  13. Remove the foil from the baking dish, and continue baking for another 25 minutes or until the cheese on top is melted and bubbly.
  14. Remove the lasagna from the oven, and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
